An Extremely Brief Introduction to SSH

03.05.24

Congratulations! You made it! Which means you are excited and ready to learn about the beautiful world of SSH! SSH or The Secure Shell Protocol is a method for securely sending commands to a computer over an unsecured network. It uses cryptography to authenticate and encrypt the connection between devices. In short, we want to have secure access to our remote servers right? We need to manage our servers from afar right? Well SSH is the solution.

Open up your terminal and type:

ssh username@desiredipaddress
