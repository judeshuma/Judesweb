BLOG

I decided after much debate to just start over. I kicked my Wordpress and purchased a Linux server. I wanted to build a website from nothing. My journey in music production has been nothing short of "Figure it out as I go". And programming has a lot of parallels in that regard. Somewhere along the way I came up with the idea for Chordo.

Which lead me to learning Python, then Flask, DNS, WSGI... It's like I knew what I was getting into...but was also forcing a bit of naivety. I started to write about all of this because I love writing, so why not? Maybe I'll tap into some of those old psychology books while I'm at it. Right though, back to the code stuff. Programming is a lot like music. You start creating something and then you break it down. There's harmony in there somewhere just like there's harmony in one's code. Everything needs to fit just right. Microscopic details can't go unnoticed. Oh and also like music it's a whole lot of back and forthing. Like a whole whole lot -- bunch of back and forthing.
