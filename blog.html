I decided after much debate to just start over. I kicked
my Wordpress and purchased a Linux server.

I'd learn HTML, CSS, and Javascript and build a site from nothing.
And then subsequently get into Python to create Chordo.
And then learn Flask to turn it into a webapp.
And then DNS and WSGI to route my server to my domain.
It's like I knew what I was getting into...but also forcing a bit of naivety.
It's a lot like music. You start creating something and then you break it down.
There's harmony in code. You create and then test,
modify and then test listen back and then test.
It's a forever loop of back and forth. Programming
can be a lot like that. A whole lot of back and forthing.
And then test, refine, then test, and modify. The search for the Holy Grail.
