Free Resources For Learning Linux

02.25.24

This post is going to be brief. I wanted to share a couple of the resources that were shared with me when I wanted to learn Linux. Karma is something I wholeheartedly believe in so I want to pass it on.

There were two things:

  • Free Intro To Linux Course
  • Free Virtual Machine To Run Linux On Your Mac

You can easily enroll in the course and download the VM to immediately get started. I will admit the VM is nowhere near as efficient as Parallels but I like the idea of getting a better understanding of something before throwing hard earned dollars at it. Maybe Linux isn't for you. These two resources are a great way to find out. Happy Coding.
