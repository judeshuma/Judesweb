ABOUTME

Hello, my name is Jude Shuma. I am an inherently curious individual from Chicago, IL. I am proud to say I have a bachelors degree in English Literature. My life has been far from convention which is probably why I am here writing this now. I have pursued art for the majority of my adult life. At one point I was working four different jobs on a weekly basis to fund the build out of my recording studio. I still own and operate that studio to this day. I have written music for commercials and movies. Landed my songs on multiple major American Television Networks. I even learned to daytrade the stock market on my own to allow more time and freedom to make art. I found later that the stress outweighed the reward...not for me. But hey it was a great learning experience. And I did manage to put away some decent capital. I have found love by walking up to a complete stranger 9 years ago.
